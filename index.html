Tekstiä

Fallout 2 is a post-apocalyptic computer role-playing game that was developed and published by Black Isle Studios in 1998 as a sequel to Interplay's Fallout.
The game takes place in the year 2241 on the West Coast of what used to be the United States, mostly in California and Nevada.
